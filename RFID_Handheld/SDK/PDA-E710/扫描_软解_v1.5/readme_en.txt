There are two ways to show how to scan the datas :

1. Enter the scan data directly at the cursor (Set the output mode into "Input Box Input" in the Scanning Service software)
2. Obtain the data from the Application , it is called Integrated development (Set the output mode into "Broadcast Receive" in the Scanning Service software) , for specific receiving methods, please check the sample application and the development introduction in the same folder .
